Negotiation Tactics

Shell Tactics

  • Strategic Preparation: Complete value analysis and research on Davis Parker's past cases
  • Crafting Powerful Values: Focus on customer safety while maintaining financial viability
  • Shaping Perception: Present ourselves as committed to doing right by customers

Voss Tactics

  • Tactical Empathy: Acknowledge the legitimacy of customer concerns
  • Calibrated Questions: "How would a high-profile trial help your clients?"
  • "That's Right" Moment: Get Davis to acknowledge the benefits of quick resolution

Cialdini Principles

  • Reciprocity: Offer immediate recall initiation before discussing compensation
  • Commitment & Consistency: Get incremental agreements on smaller issues
  • Social Proof: Highlight other successful class-action settlements
  • Scarcity: Time-limited settlement offer with fixed compensation pool

Anchor and Concessions Plan

  1. Initial anchor: Immediate recall + $50M compensation fund for verified damages
  2. First concession: Add loaner vehicles for highest-risk vehicles
  3. Second concession: Increase compensation to $150M with tiered distribution
  4. Third concession: Public statement acknowledging issue (not a full apology)
  5. Final position: $300M compensation + full recall with loaners + public statement
